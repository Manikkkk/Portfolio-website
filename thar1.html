Thar DriveMax Landing Page

Project Overview

Thar DriveMax is a dark-themed landing page for tire, wheel, and automotive accessories. The design emphasizes a premium and rugged look with bold visuals, clean typography, and high contrast. The goal was to create a landing page that captures attention and drives engagement for vehicle enthusiasts looking for performance upgrades.

Key Features

  • Hero section with impactful vehicle imagery for instant brand recall
  • Category cards (Tires, Wheels, Accessories) for quick exploration
  • Modern card-based layout to highlight product features and benefits
  • Fully responsive design for desktop and mobile users
  • Dark theme with gradients and highlights for a premium automotive feel
  • Contact & support section with clear CTAs to boost user interaction

Technologies Used

  • Figma (UI design, prototyping, component creation)
  • Design systems & grids for layout consistency
  • Typography & color styles for theme management
  • Interactive prototyping for navigation flow

My Role & Learnings

  • Designed and developed the UI/UX layout from scratch
  • Crafted dark-themed color schemes for premium appeal
  • Implemented responsive cards and sections with accessibility in mind
  • Learned to balance aesthetics with clarity for automotive product websites
