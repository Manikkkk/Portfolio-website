Sneaker E-commerce App

Project Overview

Drops is a modern sneaker shopping mobile app concept designed to provide a smooth and stylish shopping experience for sneaker enthusiasts. The design focuses on a clean, minimal interface with strong product visuals, intuitive navigation, and engaging call-to-actions to help users browse, explore, and purchase sneakers effortlessly.

Key Features

  • Home screen with search bar, brand filters, and promotional banners
  • New Arrival section showcasing trending sneakers with wishlist option
  • Product detail page with high-quality images, price, stock status, and reviews
  • Size selection and Add to Cart/Buy Now options for easy checkout
  • Wishlist and cart indicators for user convenience

Technologies Used

  • Figma (UI design, prototyping, and component creation)
  • Design grids & spacing system for consistent layout
  • Typography & iconography for clean visual hierarchy
  • Interactive prototyping to simulate shopping and checkout flows

My Role & Learnings

  • Designed the UI/UX flow for a shopping app from browsing to checkout
  • Focused on creating a minimal and modern e-commerce experience
  • Built reusable components (cards, buttons, product previews) for scalability
  • Enhanced my skills in mobile app design patterns and user-centered interactions
  • Learned to balance visual appeal with functional usability in mobile UI design
