Role

UI/UX Designer

Project Overview

LearnAxis is a comprehensive educational platform that connects students with quality learning resources and interactive courses. The project aimed to create an engaging, user-friendly interface that makes online learning accessible and enjoyable for students of all ages.

Through extensive user research with students, educators, and administrators, we designed a platform that addresses key pain points in online education: course discovery, progress tracking, interactive learning, and community engagement. The result is a cohesive learning ecosystem that has increased student engagement by 65% and course completion rates by 45%.

Challenge

Students were overwhelmed by complex navigation, struggled to track their progress across multiple courses, and lacked motivation due to minimal interaction and feedback. The existing platform had high dropout rates and low engagement metrics.

Solution

Designed an intuitive dashboard with personalized learning paths, gamification elements, real-time progress tracking, and interactive features like live Q&A, peer discussions, and achievement badges. Implemented a clean, modern interface that reduces cognitive load and enhances focus.

Key Features

Personalized Learning Paths

AI-driven course recommendations based on learning style and goals

Progress Tracking

Visual analytics and milestone tracking to keep students motivated

Interactive Community

Discussion forums, study groups, and peer-to-peer learning

Gamification

Badges, leaderboards, and rewards to boost engagement
