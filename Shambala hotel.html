Shambala Luxury Hotel Website

Project Overview

Shambala Hotel & Resort is a luxury-themed hotel booking website concept designed to provide a premium digital experience for travelers. The project emphasizes elegance, clarity, and functionality, enabling users to easily explore room collections, discover accommodations, and access booking details. The design uses a light, sophisticated theme with strong visuals of hotel rooms and facilities, creating a welcoming and professional feel for potential guests.

Key Features

  • Hero section with featured hotel image and clear call-to-action for booking
  • Room collections carousel with pricing, amenities, and quick booking options
  • Explore Rooms section showcasing different categories with visuals and details
  • Offers & Accommodations highlights for premium rooms and packages
  • FAQ section for quick answers to common guest questions
  • Footer with Discover, Experiences, and Contact information

Technologies Used

  • Figma (UI design, prototyping, and layout creation)
  • Design grids & spacing system for alignment consistency
  • Typography & color styles for a clean, luxurious theme
  • Interactive prototyping to demonstrate navigation flow and booking process

My Role & Learnings

  • Designed the complete UI layout and page flow in Figma
  • Focused on creating a luxury brand experience through visuals and typography
  • Built reusable components for room cards, FAQ toggles, and navigation
  • Enhanced skills in designing for hospitality websites with user-centric booking journeys
  • Learned to balance aesthetic appeal with usability in a professional web design
